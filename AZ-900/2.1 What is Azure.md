# Azure

A continually expanding set of cloud services to meet business challenges.

## What Does Azure Offer?

- **Be ready for the future** - continuous innovation
- **Build on your terms** - build and deploy how you want
- **Operate hybrid seamlessly** - integrate and manage your environment with tools and services designed for a hybrid cloud solution
- **Trust your cloud** - get security from the ground up

## What Can I Do with Azure?

Azure provides more than 100 services that enable you to run your existing applications on virtual machines, and explore intelligent bots and mixed reality.
